Huevos Rancheros

Note: this recipe is a work in progress, I have not adjusted the amounts, proofed the text, or anything -- I haven’t tested it at all!

Sauce

  • 2 c. diced green peppers
  • 2 c. diced onions
  • olive oil
  • 2 t. chili powder (more if you want)
  • 1 T. ground cumin
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 t. oregano
  • 1 t. black pepper
  • 1-8 oz. can tomato sauce
  • 1-16 oz. can diced or crushed tomates
  • dash salt
  1. In heavy sauce pan saute onions & green peppers in olive oil.
  2. Add spices and canned tomato products
  3. Simmer a bit and add salt to taste.

Serve hot over eggs with beans or anything else!
