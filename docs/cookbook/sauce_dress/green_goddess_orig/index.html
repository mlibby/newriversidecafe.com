GREEN GODDESS DRESSING

Makes 2 cups

  • 1/4 c. cucumber
  • 1/2 c. Green peppers
  • 1/4 c. parsley
  • 1/4 c. scallions
  • 1 c. sour cream
  • pinch of salt
  • 3/4 t. pepper
  • 3/4 t. dill

Puree top four ingrediants. Add sour cream. Add spices. Mix well, and chill. Best served with a cold salad.
